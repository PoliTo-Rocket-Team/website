PoliTo Rocket Team

Born for Space

Who are we?

We are the newly established rocketry student team of Politecnico di Torino, entirely dedicated to the design and construction of high-powered sounding rockets.

The people behind it are engineering students from different courses with great plans for the future.

Our timeline

October 2021

The beginning

Inspired by the USC RPL and Skyward Experimental Rocketry, six students started designing a solid-propelled rocket

June 2022

Team approval

Politecnico di Torino officially approved the team, which in the meantime had expanded and greeted new students

January 2023

Cavour launch

Planned launch of our first rocket: Cavour
